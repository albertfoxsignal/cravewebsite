Brand Partner Case Study

What a branded
CraveBot looks like

Live Deployment
USC Location
1 Machine deployed
24/7 Availability

Sukhi's — a Bay Area–based Indian food brand — partnered with Crave Robotics to expand distribution onto the USC campus through a fully branded CraveBot kiosk. The machine features a custom vinyl wrap in Sukhi's brand colors and serves their authentic frozen-to-ready Indian meals to students and staff around the clock.

With no kitchen requirements and zero operational burden on USC, the deployment gave Sukhi's immediate access to a high-density captive audience — a channel that would otherwise require a full food service buildout.
