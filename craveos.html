01 Analytics Dashboard

Live revenue &performance data

🤖

02 Machine Monitoring

Every machine,always in view

CraveOS tracks the operational status of every deployed unit in real time — surfacing alerts before they become problems and keeping the fleet at 96% uptime.

🟢
Live online / offline status
Real-time connection status per machine with last-active timestamps and automatic alert escalation.
⚠️
Low stock & error alerts
Automated notifications when inventory drops below threshold or a machine encounters a hardware fault.
🕐
Operating hours management
Configure per-machine active hours, track revenue per hour, and flag machines with unexpected downtime.
03 Content Management

Menu, partners &campaigns — one place

CraveOS lets operators manage what's served, to whom, and how it's promoted — without touching a line of code or filing a support ticket.

🍱
Per-machine menu control
Enable or disable individual products by machine. Manage pricing, labels, and product order from a central console.
🏷️
Coupon & campaign management
Create promo codes, set discount types, track redemptions, and measure campaign ROI by location and SKU.
🤝
Partner portal & scoped views
Provision token-based partner dashboards with machine-level filtering, custom branding, and hidden tabs.
📣
SMS campaign integration
Connect Avochato campaigns directly to redemption data — see open rates, engagement, and promo-driven sales.

04 Logistics & Planning

Cold chain, routes,and replenishment

CraveOS gives operations teams full visibility into inventory levels, restocking schedules, and cold chain status — so no machine runs empty and no route is wasted.

📦
Inventory tracking per machine
Live stock levels per SKU per machine. Color-coded thresholds trigger restock alerts before a machine runs out.
🚚
Restock route planning
Plan and optimise delivery routes across the fleet. Assign stops, track driver progress, and log completed restocks.
❄️
Cold chain compliance
Temperature logging and chain-of-custody tracking ensures every frozen meal meets food safety standards.
🗓️
Demand forecasting
Historical sales data drives automated replenishment suggestions — order the right quantity, at the right time.
