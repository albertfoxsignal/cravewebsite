About Us

Smart food service,reinvented

✦
Founded by Paul Chen, creator of Botrista Technology — bringing deep operational experience in food automation to every CraveBot deployment.

Crave Robotics provides ready-to-eat smart vending meals in under 3 minutes — servicing U.S. captive venues including university campuses, hotels, hospitals, and transit centers.

Our turnkey solution covers all day-parts: breakfast, snacks, desserts, and dinner entrees stored frozen and replenished via our cold chain logistics network.
